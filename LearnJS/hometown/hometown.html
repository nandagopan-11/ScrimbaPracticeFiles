A L A P P U Z H A

Alappuzha, known as the "Venice of the East," has a rich maritime history and is now known for its boat races, backwater vacations, beaches, marine products and coir industry. The Alappuzha Beach is a popular spot for picnics and features a 137-year-old pier. The beach is also home to the Vijaya Beach Park, which includes entertainment facilities and an old lighthouse that adds to its attraction.
